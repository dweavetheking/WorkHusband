About The Author

Torica Tymes

Torica Tymes is a talented author known for his compelling African American romance and drama novels that explore complex relationships, personal struggles, and the resilience of the human spirit. With his finger on the pulse of contemporary issues affecting the Black community, Tymes weaves stories that are both entertaining and thought-provoking.

His latest novel, "Falling For My Work Husband," delves into the life of Jamil and Nicole as they navigate love, financial hardship, and moral dilemmas. Tymes' authentic storytelling and rich character development have earned him a dedicated following of readers who eagerly anticipate each new release.
